# Privacy Policy for Unheard

Last updated: April 2026

## Overview
Unheard is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
This app does not collect any personal information. All audio processing happens locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Offline Functionality
Unheard works entirely offline. No internet connection is required, and no data is transmitted to external servers.
